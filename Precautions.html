Precautions




  1. Seperation at the source :

    2. Encourage proper seperation of waste at the source into categories like recyclables, organic waste
    and non-recyclables. This makes recycling and disposal more efficient.

    Types of Rubbish
    Type Example
    Solid Household Waste Kitchen scraps,packaging
    Liquid Household Waste cleaning waste,cooking fats, wastewater, cleaning liquids or grease
    Hazardous Waste pharmaceuticals, anything that contains mercury, solvents, some paints, aerosol cans, pool chemicals, batteries, gasoline, fuel
    Medical/Clinical Waste bandages, needles, single-use medical devices, packaging, samples, and PPE such as gloves, gowns and masks.
    Electrical Waste (E-Waste) computers and computer parts, printers, DVD and music players, TVs, telephones, vacuum cleaners

  2. Secure and safe storage :

    3. Get guarantied that waste is stored safely and securely to eliminate spilage,contamination and access
    by pests. Use sealed containers for storing waste, especially organic waste.

  3. Use proper disposal methodologies :

    4. Learn individuals about proper disposal methods for different kinds of waste. Hazardous materials like batteries, electronics, and chemicals should be disposed of at designated collection points rather than thrown in the regular trash.

  4. Minimize poisonous materials :

    5. Reduce the use of toxic substances in households, such as harsh cleaning chemicals and pesticides. Opt for environmentally friendly alternatives whenever possible.

  5. Making compost

    6. You can minimize the amount of organic ways by composting. As compost is very rich nutrient soil, you can use it for gardening.

  6. Proper Disposal of Medical Waste:
    7. Handle medical waste, such as used syringes and expired medications, with care. Ensure they are disposed of properly according to local regulations to prevent contamination and health risks.

  7. Reduce Packaging Waste:
    8. Use products with minimal amount of packaging. Select products with bio degradable packages. Prevent using single-use plastic packagings.

  8. Awareness and Education:
    9. By organizing campaings, make the people educated about waste management and environmental conservation.

  9. Collecting regularly and wastage :
    10. Ensure that waste is disposing regularly and collected by municipal authorities according to rules and regulations. Encourage participation in community cleanup initiatives.

  10. Reuse and Donate:
    11. You can resuse your waste like clothes and water bottles. And also you can donate it to someone who found it usefull.
